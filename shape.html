How It Works
👁️
Real-Time Detection
MediaPipe Hands tracks 21 landmarks on your hand — including all 5 fingertip positions — directly in your browser. No data is sent to any server.
✋
Grip Shape Analysis
We measure the width profile of your hand at multiple heights — from fingertips to palm — to create a shape signature that matches how you naturally hold a mouse.
🖱️
Shape Matching
Your grip profile is compared against real measured specs of 898 gaming mice from 50+ brands. Mice with the closest shape to your hand rise to the top.
